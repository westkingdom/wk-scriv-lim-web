    What Awards get a scroll? Interested in helping out? Here are a few suggestions!

    Become a Scribe. It really is a lot easier than you think, and it's fun! If you are new to the West (or returning) it's a great way meet people and make new friends.

    Become an Administrative Deputy. There are a currently a number of assistance positions that require no scribing at all. For example: A Warden, to educate the populace (especially new award recipients) as to the Company's policies and procedures; or be an Autocrat for workshops, scriptoria and scribal displays.

    Become a Patron. Do you have a workspace large enough to hold a class or a weekly scriptorium? Do you have art supplies that you could donate? Would you be willing to hold a fundraiser?
